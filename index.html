8 Ways to Talk to Someone at UNITED Airlines

Need urgent help? Call 1 (833) 489-6266 or 1-800-UNITED (24/7 Support)

Choose the method that suits you best to contact a live representative at UNITED Airlines.

Phone Support

Call 1 (833) 489-6266 or 1-800-UNITED. Say "agent" or press "0" after the prompts.

Live Chat

Access the Live Chat option on the UNITED Airlines website under the Help section.

Mobile App

Use the FlyUNITED app to chat or call support easily while you're on the go.

Email Support

Send non-urgent queries via email for detailed responses and written confirmation.

Social Media

Message UNITED Airlines on Facebook or Twitter for quick answers and updates.

Boarding Pass Requests

Call 1 (833) 489-6266 to request a duplicate pass or get help printing one.

Travel Requirements

Call to learn about travel restrictions, entry rules, and required documents for stopovers.

Seat Upgrade Inquiries

Speak with a live agent to check eligibility, availability, and pricing for upgrades.

Pro Tips to Reach a Live Agent Faster

  • Call during off-peak hours (late evenings or early mornings).
  • Say “agent” or press “0” after the automated prompts.
  • Have your booking info ready for faster service.

Need More Help?

You can also reach out for help regarding flight changes, refunds, cancellations, hotel bookings and more via the above methods.
